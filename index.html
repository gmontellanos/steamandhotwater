About Us

We are a licensed, insured, and bonded C-4 contractor. We have over 20 years of experience with service and repair of steam and hydronic heating systems in the City of San Francisco. We are experienced with single pipe and two pipe steam, radiant floor, hydronic baseboard, hydronic radiator, and hydronic air handers. We are experienced with service and repair of various boiler manufacturers. We strongly believe that routine regular maintenance will prolong the life of the entire heating system. However, we only service residential heating systems.

We are also are certified backflow testers. We handle all the paperwork with the City of San Francisco. With over 15 years of backflow testing and repair experience we will ensure that your backflow preventer will maintain the high level of protection that is required by the City of San Francisco.

FAQs

Do you service commercial steam and hydronic heating systems?

Unfortunately, we do not. We only service single family residential homes. We do not service heating systems for apartments, multi-unit buildings, commercial or any other commercial-style heating systems.

Do you service heating systems for condominiums?

It depends. If the boiler and heating system belongs to the individual condominium, then yes. However, if the heating system is connected to the centralized heating system for the entire complex, then unfortunately we will not service that heating system.

Do you test backflow preventers for residential and commercial buildings?

Yes, we do.

Do you provide after hours or emergency services?

Unfortunately, we do not unless it is a heating system that was initially installed by us.

Do you provide service outside of San Francisco?

Unfortunately, we do not work outside of the City and County of San Francisco.

What is the best way to schedule an appointment?

Feel free to call, text, or email. However, the best way to schedule a visit is by email. Emails are checked constantly and we do respond promptly via email.

What is your warranty?

We provide the industry standard one year warranty for the repair.
